Lesson target

The teacher edition starts this lesson by building the meaning of multiplication from equal groups. The symbols come after the picture and words make sense.

First See equal groups
Then Write repeated addition
Then Write multiplication

Notice

Instead of counting every arm by ones, count by the group size: 2, 4, 6, 8, ...

Unit form

Unit form names the repeated unit.

6 twos = 12

This sits between repeated addition and multiplication in the teacher edition.

Picture 6 equal groups with 2 counters in each group
Addition 2 + 2 + 2 + 2 + 2 + 2 = 12
Unit form 6 twos = 12
Multiplication 6 x 2 = 12
