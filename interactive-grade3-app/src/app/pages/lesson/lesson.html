Multiplication and Division Vocabulary

In multiplication, we say times.

Example: 3 times 4 means 3 groups of 4.

The answer to a multiplication problem is called the product.

Example: 3 × 4 = 12, so 12 is the product.

In division, we say divided by.

Example: 12 divided by 3 means 12 is shared into 3 equal groups.

The answer to a division problem is called the quotient.

Example: 12 ÷ 3 = 4, so 4 is the quotient.
